Privacy Policy

Welcome to Palm Travel Bookings! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information (such as name, email address, phone number) provided when making a booking or inquiry.
  • Travel preferences and special requirements.
  • Payment information for processing transactions.
  • Information collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Process and manage your bookings and reservations.
  • Communicate with you regarding your travel arrangements and provide customer support.
  • Personalize your experience and provide tailored recommendations.
  • Improve our services, website functionality, and user experience.
  • Conduct analytics and research to understand trends and customer preferences.

3. Information Sharing

We may share your personal information with:

  • Travel service providers (airlines, hotels, car rental companies) to facilitate your bookings.
  • Third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business (such as payment processors, customer support providers).
  • Legal and regulatory authorities when required by law or to protect our rights and interests.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Withdraw your consent for certain data processing activities.
  • Request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal requirements.
  • Opt-out of receiving marketing communications.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at palmtravelbookings.ja. or at Tele (876) 879-0878.

By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
